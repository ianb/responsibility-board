How to Play

Responsibility Board!

A Jeopardy-style game to learn about adult responsibilities.

Gameplay

  1. Click on any question cell to reveal the clue
  2. Players buzz in by pressing their number key (1-5)
  3. First player to buzz in can answer
  4. Click "Show Answer" to reveal the correct answer
  5. Judge the response: Correct (adds points), Incorrect (subtracts points), or Pass (no change)

Controls

  • Setup Players: Configure player names and number of players
  • New Game: Start fresh with new random categories and reset scores
  • Reset Board: Reshuffle categories and make all questions available again

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • 1-5: Buzz in as Player 1-5
  • Space: Show answer (when question is displayed)
  • Enter: Mark answer as correct
  • Backspace: Mark answer as incorrect
  • Escape: Close modal

Scoring

  • Questions are worth $200, $400, $600, $800, or $1000
  • Correct answer: Add points to your score
  • Incorrect answer: Subtract points from your score
  • Pass: No points awarded
